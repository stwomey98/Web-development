About Cit's legoland

Hello and welcome to our legoland, where you can see and do lots of different activities around the campus,
such as building your own lego man or you could go see lego buildings of computers and a lifesize version of the president in lego form.
There is many different areas on the campus that you can see the lego structures, the vast makority of them are being shown in the art gallery as the design is top notch.
Here is a video of some work done with lego on youtube. There are many students that are in our group of builders many of which
have loved lego since they were children and some that have just gotten into it and there has been many new friendships formed because of this.
We welcome anyone else that would like to join and people that do join can get a mini figure of themselves made. Our club will be five years old as of july 20th so we hope to see you there.
